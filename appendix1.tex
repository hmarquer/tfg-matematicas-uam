\subsection{El operador laplaciano}

\transclude{laplaciano}

En $\mathbb{D}$, el \exhyperref[defn:laplaciano]{laplaciano}{operador laplaciano} $\Delta \colon \mathcal{C}^2 \left(\mathbb{D}\right) \to \mathcal{C}\left(\mathbb{D}\right)$ viene dado por
\[
\forall z \in \mathbb{D} : \Delta f(z) = \pdv[2]{f}{x}(z) + \pdv[2]{f}{y}(z) \quad \tex{donde } z = x + iy. 
\]
